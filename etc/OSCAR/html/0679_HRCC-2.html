Instrument:  HRCC-2 

Instrument details
Acronym HRCC-2
Full name High-Resolution CCD Camera -2
Purpose High-resolution land and vegetation observation
Short description 5 channels in VIS and NIR (1 panchromatic) [see detailed characteristics below]
Background Evolution of HRCC on CBERS-1, CBERS-2 and CBERS-2B
Scanning Technique Pushbroom. 12,000 pixel/line (PAN), 6000 pixel/line (multispectral), swath 60 km, possible to be pointed within a field of regard of about 900 km
Resolution 5 m (PAN), 10 m (multispectral)
Coverage / Cycle Global coverage in 45 days or 3 days by exploiting strategic pointing
