Instrument:  IMG 

Instrument details
Acronym IMG
Full name Interferometric Monitor for Greenhouse gases
Purpose Atmospheric chemistry. Tracked species: CH4, CO2, H2O, N2O and O3
Short description IR interferometer, spectral range 3.3-14.0 µm (714-3030 cm-1) split in 3 bands [see detailed characteristics below]
Background New development
Scanning Technique Nadir viewing. Along-track sampling at 13 s intervals (86 km)
Resolution 8 km
Coverage / Cycle Global in 1.5 months for cells of 100 km side (duty cycle: 3 days each 13 days)
Mass 130 kg Power 150 W Data Rate 882 kbps

 

Providing Agency METI
Instrument Maturity Flown on an R&D satellite
Utilization Period: 1996 to 1997-06-30
Last update: 2018-03-19
