Instrument:  CAPI 

Instrument details
Acronym CAPI
Full name Cloud and Aerosol Polarization Imager
Purpose Image mask and optical depth of clouds and aerosol
Short description 5-channel UV/VIS/NIR/SWIR radiometer with three polarisations in two channels, and resolution changing with channel [see datails in the table below]
Background Support to the CO2 measurements from the TANSAT Atmospheric Carbon-dioxide Grating Spectrometer (ACGS)
Scanning Technique Pushbroom, cross-track swath ~200 km, operated within a solar zenith angle < 65 degree
Resolution 250 or 1000 m at s.s.p. (see table)
Coverage / Cycle Global in two weeks
Mass 80 kg Power 70 W Data Rate

 

Providing Agency MOST
Instrument Maturity Flown on an R&D satellite
Utilization Period: 2017 to ≥2019
