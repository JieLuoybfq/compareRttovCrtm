Instrument:  GLI 

Instrument details
Acronym GLI
Full name Global Imager
Purpose Multipurpose VIS/IR imagery
Short description 36-channel spectroradiometer covering the range 0.38-12.0 ?m (29 channels in UV/VIS/NIR, 7 in MWIR/TIR). 18 channels have 10 nm bandwidth, for ocean colour [see detailed characteristics below]
Background Evolution of OCTS flown on ADEOS
Scanning Technique Wiskbroom, 1600 km swath, capability of ± 20° off-pointing along track to avoid sunglint
Resolution 250 m s.s.p. for 6 VNIR channels, 1 km s.s.p. for the other 30 channels
Coverage / Cycle Global each day for LW channels, each two days for SW channels
Mass 450 kg Power 400 W Data Rate 4.1 Mbps

 

Providing Agency JAXA
Instrument Maturity Flown on an R&D satellite
Utilization Period: 2003 to 2003-10-25
Last update: 2013-12-04
