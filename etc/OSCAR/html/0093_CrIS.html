Instrument:  CrIS 

Instrument details
Acronym CrIS
Full name Cross-track Infrared Sounder
Purpose Temperature/humidity sounding, coarse ozone profile and total-column green-house gases
Short description Interferometer with three IR bands, 1305 channels in initial operation mode on SNPP [see detailed characteristics below]. Future operation mode on SNPP and JPSS will have 2211 channels with the same full spectral resolution in all three bands.
Background New development
Scanning Technique Cross-track: 32 steps of 48 km s.s.p., swath 2200 km - Along-track: one 48-km line every 8 s
Resolution 3 x 3 14 km IFOV covering a 48 x 48 km2 cell (average sampling distance: 16 km)
Coverage / Cycle Near-global coverage twice/day
Mass 165 kg Power 123 W Data Rate 1.5 Mbps

 

Providing Agency NASA
Instrument Maturity Flown on operational programme
Utilization Period: 2012-03-06 to ≥2038
Last update: 2018-03-06
