Instrument:  OLS 

Instrument details
Acronym OLS
Full name Operational Linescan Systerm
Purpose Cloud observation and day/night VIS imagery for fire detection
Short description 3-channel VIS/IR radiometer, including a day/night VIS channel implemented by a photomultiplier [see detailed characteristics below]
Background Flown on all satellites of the DMSP series
Scanning Technique Whiskbroom; 7325 pixels/line sampled cross-track for panoramic distortion correction at 0.5 km intervals. Swath 2960 km
Resolution 0.56 km. Cross-track sampling at 0.5 km intervals
Coverage / Cycle Global coverage twice/day in both VIS and TIR
Mass 25 kg Power 170 W Data Rate 266 kbps

 

Providing Agency DoD
Instrument Maturity Flown on operational programme
Utilization Period: 1976 to ≥2019
Last update: 2016-10-14
