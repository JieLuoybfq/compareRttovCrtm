Instrument:  HRV 

Instrument details
Acronym HRV
Full name Haute Résolution dans le Visible
Purpose High-resolution land and vegetation observation.  Digital Elevation Model (DEM)
Short description Two parallel radiometers, 4 VIS/NIR channels, three multi-spectral (MS), one panchromatic (PAN) [see detailed characteristics below]
Background Flown on SPOT-1 (1986-2003), SPOT-2 (1990-2009), SPOT-3 (1993-1996)
Scanning Technique Pushbroom, 3000 pixel/line (MS), 6000 pixel/line (PAN), swath 60 km ssp (117 km with two instruments), cross-track pointing capability within a range of ± 450 km.  Stereoscopic capability between successive orbits
Resolution 20 m (MS), 10 m (PAN)
Coverage / Cycle Global coverage in 26 days, in daylight.  With strategic pointing, one place can be observed every 3 days.
Mass Power Data Rate 50 Mbps

 

Providing Agency CNES
Instrument Maturity Flown on operational programme
Utilization Period: 1986 to 2009-07-01
Last update: 2017-03-28
