Instrument:  SAR RCM 

Instrument details
Acronym SAR RCM
Full name Synthetic Aperture Radar (C-band) for RadarSat constellation
Purpose High-resolution all-weather multi-purpose imager for ocean, land and ice
Short description C-band SAR, frequency 5.4 GHz, multi-polarisation and variable pointing/resolution [see detailed characteristics below]
Background Evolution of SAR on RadarSat-1 and RadarSat-2
Scanning Technique Side-looking, variable pointing within a 500-km-wide field of regard (20-49° off-nadir), to either left- or right- hands alternatively; swath 20 to 500 km, depending on operation mode - See table
Resolution 3 to 100 m, depending on operation mode - See table
Coverage / Cycle Near-global coverage in 12 days for the ‘low resolution’ mode (duty cycle 12 %); 4 days for the 3-satellite constellation; longer periods for other operation modes; shorter on selected areas by strate
Mass 600 kg Power 1270 W Data Rate
