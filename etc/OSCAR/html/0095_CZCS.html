Instrument:  CZCS 

Instrument details
Acronym CZCS
Full name Coastal Zone Colour Scanner
Purpose Ocean colour monitoring
Short description 5 narrow-band channels in VIS/NIR + 1 TIR channel [see detailed characteristics below]
Background New development, prototype of instruments for ocean colour monitoring
Scanning Technique Mechanical, cross-track, 1600 km swath
Resolution 825 m s.s.p.
Coverage / Cycle Near-global coverage every day, in daylight
Mass 27 kg Power 11.4 W Data Rate 800 kps

 

Providing Agency NASA
Instrument Maturity Flown on an R&D satellite
Utilization Period: 1979 to 1994-08-01
Last update: 2012-11-29
