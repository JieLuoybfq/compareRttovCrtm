Instrument:  ACGS 

Instrument details
Acronym ACGS
Full name Atmospheric Carbon-dioxide Grating Spectrometer
Purpose Measurement of total-column CO2
Short description Three-band NIR/SWIR grating spectroradiometer, Band-1: O2 around 760 nm; Band-2: CO2 around 1610 nm (weak band); Band-3: CO2 around 2060 nm (strong band). 2000 channels total [see details in the table below]
Background Development after the Orbiting Carbon Observatory (OCO) flown on OCO-2
Scanning Technique Pushbroom, cross-track. Swath 18 km, 9 super-pixels per line. Three pointing modes: nadir, sun glint, and target within a solar zenith angle < 65 degree
Resolution 2 km (cross track) x 3 km (along track) at s.s.p.
Coverage / Cycle Global in 5 months; less by strategic pointing
Mass 180 kg Power 170 W Data Rate
